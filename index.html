Vision & Manifesto

Stewardship over ownership.

The Yehoshama Foundation is dedicated to building resilient, unexploitative software protocols and tools that empower individuals to own their data and protect their digital footprint.

We operate under strict principles: we will never exploit the user. We ensure an "analogue fallback"â€”systems must gracefully degrade to paper or human-readable formats if complex technology fails. Everything we build will eventually be open-sourced, returning control to those who use it.

We believe in acknowledging God's role and holding to principles of faith, maintaining a subtle integration of truth without being overtly preachy. We are building for humans, guided by purpose.
